What are the detailed benefits of individual steps of Morning Ritual?

A basic morning ritual should look like this :-

1. Silence:

Did you ever notice the essence and power of silence during the day? If not, then you are missing out on something very powerful. After you wake up, take your mind to either a complete silent zone or indulge into spirituality for 5 minutes.

Benefit – It restricts your mind of splurging out unnecessary thoughts right from your morning. This helps you focus on more important things!
 
2. Start Working out:

If you are a night owl, you should do this to keep you woke up during the early hours of the morning.

Not much just 7 minutes of stretching out would do the job. All you have to make sure about that is to be regular in it and not to quit in between.

Benefit – It sets your mind and body aligned and helps your brain in functioning more.
 
3. Cold Showers:

Don’t wait for long to take a shower, just do it after your workout. Also, remember that you do not want to spend more than 5 minutes in taking a shower every morning. Also, never pamper yourself to sleep with warm water but splash cold water on yourself.

Benefit – This is one of the best morning charges that you could give to yourself. It makes your sleep or tiredness go away in what 5 minutes?
 
4. Meditate:

If you haven’t already, start meditating in a routine. You will get to know the power of focusing on something and also will feel up for the most part of the day.

For starters, you can start meditating for 10 minutes a day or even less and try to concentrate on different things.

Benefit – Keeps you active, puts your focus on what is important and eliminates toxic thoughts.
 
5. Hot Coffee/ Green Tea:

To stimulate your metabolism and to help you charge yourself, getting a cup of something hot is very important.

Grab your favorite mug after your shower and pour in your favorite hot beverage to get started with the day. It should take you 2 minutes to do so.

Benefit – Increases your metabolism, keep you awake for long hours and increases concentration.
 
6. Read:

Take a motivational novel and start reading it for 15 minutes every day to charge yourself and bring that spin of positivity before getting along with your work. Reading something inspirational never leaves you demotivated.

Benefit – Makes you thoughtful and keeps you feeling motivated every single time.
 
7. Jot Down:

Start writing down your goals for the day and what you want to achieve till the end of the day. Believe it or not, this ritual is the most powerful of all.

Writing your goals down helps you visualize things and also helps you in prioritizing things. It should take you a maximum of 5 minutes every day.

Benefit – Makes you stick to your set of tasks, prioritize things for and also manages your time wonderfully.
