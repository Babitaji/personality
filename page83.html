Tips to Avoid Distraction

“Starve your distractions, feed your focus” is a great quote.

Distraction, the extreme agitation of the mind, is a thing that hinders a person from focussing onto a particular thing. For example, when you keep checking your mobile after every 10 minutes that is a distraction.

When you are distracted you become less productive. Distraction can cost you a lot in the long run if not avoided from the very beginning.

Distraction delays work, therefore, one should remain focussed in order to get the most out of a situation in a given time.

We need to tackle it and devise ways to remain dedicated and focussed.

Listed below are a few ways that can help you :

1 - Lightly scented candles or spray air freshener :

This will create an atmosphere of peace and will make you calmer and composite. You can then become more focused and more dedicated.

2 - Move to a quiet place :

Often new places distract from concentration power. Therefore, if at any place, you find it impossible to concentrate, then moving from that place to some quieter place will help you to concentrate.

Even otherwise, if you lose focus, then moving yourself to some calmer place for a while will help you regain more concentration power.

3 - Organize things and segregate them :

You should plan ahead of time and chalk out how to proceed. Before starting something, decide the goal to be accomplished and organize things according to their level of difficulty i.e., decide on what work to do first and what comes next.

4 - Set a timer before doing work :

Before proceeding with your scheduled work, set a timer on your phone, i.e., even before starting any work, assign a specified time to finish that work. It is possible that you might fail to get your work done by the deadlines, but this will keep you focussed and will help you to avoid distractions owing to the allotted time.

5 - Keep your food near you :

The biggest distractions are hunger and thirst. Eat well and drink plenty of water. Keep food near you so that you can easily have it when you feel the need to eat. In a study, held in 2012, it was found that mild dehydration leads to inattention and distraction.

6 - Eat healthy :

You should eat healthy food so that you remain healthy. An upset stomach will definitely distract you a lot.
