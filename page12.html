The following are the 9 major requirements toward the development of personality

1 - Kindness

2 - Cleanliness of Both mind and body

3 - Unselfishness

4 - Good Disposition

5 - Obedience to Ideals and Principles

6 - Honesty in all things

7 - Truthfulness

8 - Loyalty

9 - Good Health
