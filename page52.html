 “Sleep is the best meditation”- Dalai Lama

Life is all about working your way to the top.

Hard work, intelligence, unbreakable faith, and sweat are all that is required to reach the zenith of your career. But is it enough? How will you achieve success if you have not rested properly?

When the brain does not get much REM sleep, the neurons stop responding and that makes us dull and dreary. Here are a few tips on how to sleep better with the help of a few routine habits.

1. Create a sleep cycle:

Your body is like a clock which needs to be put into a pattern. Once you make yourself fall asleep at a certain time forcefully, after a couple of days, you will automatically feel sleepy at that time.

When you wake yourself up forcefully at 7 am, after a week, you will find yourself awake at 7 am involuntary. This way you will know that your body has established the cycle and has fallen into the schedule.

2. Exercise in the morning:

Exercising will help you activate your mind and body at the same time. It will keep you energized all day and when you finally hit the pillow, you will fall asleep immediately in no time.Practicing light yoga before bedtime will enhance your sleeping habits.

3. Avoid using phone or laptop before bedtime:

There are various things you can do instead of scrolling through your phone checking emails or messages.

You can read books, have a conversation with your loved ones, or develop a hobby. The light that comes from the screens causes our eyes to develop irritation and creates troubles when we are trying to sleep by creating ghost images once our eyes are shut.

4. Improve the environment where you sleep:

Sleeping in a peaceful calm and quiet place is an absolute necessity.

And making it a habit to sleep in a quiet environment will definitely help you in regulating your sleeping patterns.

You will find that a little difference in your sleeping environment will make a lot of difference in the quality of your sleep.
