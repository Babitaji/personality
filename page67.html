PESSIMISM: How to Ignore it?

Pessimism simply means expecting the worst out of all possible situations.

It is actually the tendency to see only the negative aspect of everything while completely ignoring its positive side.

A pessimist person will always see the glass as half empty. He ignores the vast possibilities and has a strong negative attitude towards almost everything.

A pessimist person almost always does try and influences other minds to think and feel alike.

Pessimism might seem to be a small ignorable matter to most people. But people fail to understand the huge amount of negative effect it can have on a person's life in the long run.

It can slowly push an otherwise happy person into a deep depression with continuous exposure. Thus, it is very important to understand the pessimism, its effects and how one should safeguard oneself from it.

The pessimists pass on their negative energy to other people around them. It is always advisable to maintain distance from such pessimistic thoughts, ideas, and people who believe in it.

In your day to day life, you are bound to encounter many such people. Though it is not possible to physically stay away from you, you can always ignore their school of negative thoughts.

Firstly, never let anybody snuff away your dreams or aspirations. You know your abilities best and if you are confident about it, do it.

Do not let anybody dictate to you, especially people who always see the dark side of things. Sometimes your close ones like your family and friends are too are eager to show you the bad only about a thing you are interested in.

It is not that they are not concerned about it; it is just that their own insecurities that lie unattended at the back of their mind are reflected.

Apprehensions are good to a certain extent, but fear of the subconscious mind is to be ignored.

If you feel somebody is cornering you with his pessimistic thoughts and trying to get the better side of you, simply change the conversation.

You yourself have to be certain about the thing you are pursuing or want to do. Think well about whatever you believe in and if you feel there is nothing to be insecure about, simply ignore what others says that might demoralize you.

Remember that your strong optimism is the only way to balance out any negativity that may persist.

Your goal should be loaded with only positivism. Learn to ignore pessimism, but do not ignore the person.

If you are fully committed towards your goal, one day or the other the pessimists will have to change their outlook towards you and your goals.

Till then, you just need to be polite and ignore them.
